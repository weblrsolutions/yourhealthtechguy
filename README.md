# HTC News — Health Tech Circle's News

A free, ad-free news hub for digital health, medtech, AI in healthcare, and wellness tech — the latest stories from trusted sources, aggregated in one clean place and refreshed through the day.

**Live:** https://htcnews.is-a.dev/ (also https://weblrsolutions.github.io/htcnews/)

## What it does

- Pulls health-tech headlines from trusted publishers and refreshes throughout the day
- Writes short, original summaries and tags each story by category and region
- Filters by time (24 hours / week / month), region, category, and trending keywords
- Every headline links back to the original publisher; times shown in your local timezone

## Disclaimer

Stories are aggregated from public sources. We do not own, edit, or independently verify the underlying reporting — always confirm details on the publisher's site.
